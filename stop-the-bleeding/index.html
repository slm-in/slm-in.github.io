Home Services Staff Teaching Stop The Bleeding

Pastor Kurt Erickson of Eleos Ministries and Karl Shifflett of Bikers For Christ Indiana will be departing for Ukraine around 15 March 2022 to deliver Celox™ Gauze hemostat that stops the bleeding in 3 minutes or less from serious wounds and injury.

Celox Product page: https://www.celoxmedical.com/cx-product/celox-gauze/

Once in Ukraine, we will deliver the Celox to civilians and civilian EMS, and join the civilian defense forces in the cities we will be in to help them protect their homeland from the invaders.

Karl will be remaining in Ukraine until the war is over. Pastor Kurt will be making trips back to the UK from time to time.

We have partnered with Steve Boles the Executive Director of Mission to Ukraine , and Pastor Don Louvier of Spirit Led Ministries and Bikers For Christ Indiana .

The Celox Gauze costs about $48.00 per package that can be used to save many lives.

All the money donated will be used to purchase the Celox Gauze and help with transportation costs from Warsaw, Poland to Ukrainian cities under attack.

We are working with resources inside Ukraine and Poland, to help with transporting the gauzes, Pastor Kurt, and Karl from Warsaw to the front lines.

You can follow these Facebook pages to keep up with news from inside Ukraine. For security reasons, we will not be providing specifics of our location or names of anyone we are with until the war is over. We will try to post some pictures as appropriate.

We expect to make our first round of Celox Gauze puchases around 15 March 2020. As additional funds come in after this date, we will purchase and transport them to the front line cities.
