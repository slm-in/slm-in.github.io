From The Pastor

We believe that “All scripture is breathed out by God and is profitable for teaching, reproof, for correction, and for training in righteousness, that the man of God may be competent, equipped for every good work.” 2 Timothy 3:16-17

Our goal to assist you in your walk with the Lord by helping build a personal relationship with God (The Father), Our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ (The Son), and the Spirit of God, (The Holy Spirit), and thus “equipping you for every good work.” 2 Timothy 3:17

We believe that all are held accountable, including the Pastor.

We believe that people should not be judged by their appearance or status.

We encourage everyone to be a, “fruit inspector.”

We encourage everyone to read the Word for yourselve and compare it to the teaching.

Sound Biblical Teaching For Every Day Life

Teachings are directly from the Bible.

We are commanded to “spread the Gospel to the world.” Acts 13:47-49

The Pastor’s duty is to prepare others for the work of the ministry, for the edifying of the body of Christ. Ephesians 4:12

We are all sinners and fall short of the glory of God. Romans 3:23

The only way to achieve salvation is through the acceptance of Our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ into your heart. John 14:6
