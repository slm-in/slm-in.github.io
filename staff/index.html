Pastor Don Louvier

Pastor Don is not your typical pastor in a 3-piece suit. Usually, most can find him in leather as a common wardrobe. As the State Elder for Bikers For Christ Indiana , his ministry can be compared to old time circuit preachers who traveled on horseback spreading the Word. Instead of a horse, Pastor Don usually rides a Harley, ministering at rallies, churches, and gatherings.

God has blessed his obedience in the ministry and opened doors, allowing him to preach at campgrounds, church pulpits, and many other venues.

No matter where he teaches, Pastor Don follows the command in the Bible to “spread the Gospel to all people.” He incorporates his life experiences into his teaching, bringing the Gospel into everyday life.

Pastor Don and Cyndy

Pastor Don and Cyndy Louvier have been married since 1985. They met in southern California while Don was in the U.S. Marine Corps. They currently reside in LaGrange County, Indiana and have 4 adult children.

Both Don and Cyndy became “saved” at a young age. Through the years, their walk has ranged from “walking closely” with the Lord to “fallen away” from the path.

In 2003, they both made the decision to walk the narrow path without change and become totally committed to Lord. Through the leading of the Holy Spirit, Don became an Ordained Minister in 2004.
